About Me

Hello. I am currently a Game deveolper for the US Army (VSR Techician) were I work with a team that develops large open-world games the Army uses for training. Before that I worked several contract art positions such as Environment Artist, Technicanal Artist, Charater Artist and General 3D Artist for various small indie companies.

I have also worked as Graphic Designer for multiple businesses developing company marketing material, branding for new products as well as logo designs. I have degrees in Game Development, Graphic Design and currently working on additional certifications in Game Design and Development.
